Welcome to Betta Landscaping

I started my landscaping business in 2018. Ever since the beginning I have always tried to provide the best service possible! I strive to create a win-win service that provides a job well done in a timely manner.

Here at Betta Landscaping we love to make your property look amazing. We strive to do our best, at a fair price, and on schedule. We offer lawn care, flower bed cleanups, stone walkways, sod install, weed barrier install, shrub install, shrub trimming, brush cleanups, pressure washing, and more. We are located in Moreland, GA, but we serve all of Whitewater.
