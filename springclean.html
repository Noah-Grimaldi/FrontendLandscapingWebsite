Spring & Fall Cleanup


Spring Cleanup Services

Below are the services we do to help maintain and keep your property looking the best. Our staff will achieve the goals you have for your property. We offer free estimates and service that is quality and fairly-priced.

Scalping

Removing all dead and unwanted grass from the yard. In some cases this can include dethatching.

Aeration

Perforating the soil with small holes to allow air, water and nutrients to penetrate the grass roots. This will assist root growth to be deeper and produce a stronger, more robust lawn.

Removing Debris (brush cleanups)

Are you tired of having your backyard a mess? We can clean it up to accomodate your property needs and give you a more desirable view.

Preparation of Planting Beds

Restoring mulch or pine straw (if required) in your planting bed(s). This also includes pulling weeds and removing unwanted debris.

Planting Seasonal Flowers or Plants

Planting of shrubs, annuals, or perrenials.

Weed Control for Your Flower Bed(s).

Spraying or pulling all the weeds in your flower bed(s).

Pruning Flowers, Shrubs, and Grasses.

Pruning trees or shrubs in order to keep them healthy. Includes removal of winterkill on branches and dead limbs.


Fall Cleanup Services

Multching Or Removing Leaves

Multching includes shredding leaves with the lawn mower, moving the leaves to the wood line, or removing them from the property in order to create a healthier dynamic and subdue weeds.

Garden Maintenance

Removing of all the weeds and unwanted debris.
